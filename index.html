About Us

Bakery 60 and Bistro 60, a haven in the heart of Prague, where tradition meets innovation, and every pastry is a celebration of taste. We take pride in crafting delightful treats that bring together the rich culinary heritage of Korea with a modern twist.

Our intriguing name finds its roots in the charming coincidence of language and flavors. Inspired by the Korean name for their signature milk bread – where ”6” phonetically aligns with the word for milk, and ”0” with bun – each digit in ”60” mirrors our star creation.
